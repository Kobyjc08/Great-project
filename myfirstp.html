Mountains

Colombia is plenty of big mountains because the Andins crosses in 3 parts the country,we have places very famous to do trekking and even climb in the snow all the year, our montaners counts with anything you may need to make you fell like in home. Los Nevados National Park is located in the coffee region of the central Andes of Colombia. It is known for its snowy volcanoes, glaciers, lakes and forests. The active volcano Nevado del Ruiz is in the north. Further south is the Emerald Green Lake, the trout-filled Otun Lake and the Tolima Snowy with its snow-capped summit. The park houses hummingbirds, eagles, parrots and condors. It has high wax palms and frailejones. Here you can enjoy the best adventures where you can practice sports such as hiking, climbing or even night riding ... A whole show.

Historical cities

Ciudad Perdida, also known as Teyuna or Buritaca-200, is a former Tajon indigenous village and archeological site belonging to the city of Santa Marta, in Colombia. It was built around the 8th century of our era and is considered one of the main archeological sites in Colombia.It is part of one of the more than 250 ancient villages of the four indigenous groups found on the north and southwest face of the Sierra Nevada from Santa Marta, in the department of Magdalena in northern Colombia.
